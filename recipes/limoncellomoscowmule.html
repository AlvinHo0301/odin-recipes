Limecollo Moscow Mulle

What is Limoncello Moscow Mulle

This is a fun twist on the classic Moscow Mule, using limoncello for a refreshing, lemony summertime cocktail. Use a good-quality ginger beer, such as Fever Tree®.

  • 1 cup ice
  • ½ fluid ounce fresh lime juice
  • 1½ fluid ounces limoncello liqueur
  • 1½ fluid ounces vodka
  • 5 ounces ginger beer
  • 1 thin lemon slice (optional)

Step

  1. Fill a copper mule mug with ice. Add lime juice, limoncello, and vodka; stir to combine. Top with ginger beer. Lightly stir again and garnish with lemon slice.

Cooks tips

You can use lemon juice instead of lime juice if you prefer, but I find the lime stays true to a traditional Moscow Mule.
